### Abstract: 
The aim of this paper is to highlight the role of the library in enhancing institutional NIRF ranking and focuses on the main role of the libraries and information center practices and services in support of NIRF ranking. This paper points out the impact of various library services including Management of Library and Information Services, Collection and Services Provided to Users, Extent of the use of services and Best practices etc. to perform the major role in NIRF ranking. The modern libraries are extended the library services to maintain the research quality and enhance the publications of an institute. The paper will also present the library best practices that one library can adapt to the regular library services to contribute the NIRF ranking of the institutions.
